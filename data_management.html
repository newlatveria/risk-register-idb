⚠️ Advanced Data Management

DANGER ZONE: The actions on this page are irreversible. Ensure you have exported a JSON or CSV backup before proceeding with any data deletion.

Risk Register Data Deletion (IndexedDB)

This action will delete **all risk records** permanently from your browser's IndexedDB. This is your core risk data, including all risk details, scores, and review dates.

Full System Reset (IndexedDB & Local Storage)

This will perform a complete cleanup, deleting:

  • **All Risk Records** (from IndexedDB).
  • **All Local Storage items** (including filter preferences and settings).

Use this option to fully reset the application to its initial state.
